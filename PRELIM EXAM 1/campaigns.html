Our Advocacy & Campaigns

Discover the various initiatives we lead to strengthen the nation's resilience.

Million People Run

The Million People Run is a biennial fundraising marathon held simultaneously across various cities in the Philippines. It aims to raise awareness and funds for the PRC's disaster response capabilities and the acquisition of life-saving equipment like ambulances and rescue boats.

Blood Samaritan Program

This initiative seeks to help indigent patients who need blood but cannot afford the blood processing fees. Through the generosity of corporate and individual donors, we ensure that the most vulnerable have access to safe blood when they need it most.

Red Cross 143

Our flagship volunteer program that recruits and trains 44 volunteers (1 leader and 43 members) in every barangay. This campaign ensures that every community has a dedicated team of first responders ready to report and react during any emergency.

WASH Initiative

The Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH) campaign promotes healthy practices in rural communities and schools. We build toilets, provide handwashing facilities, and educate families on proper hygiene to prevent the spread of communicable diseases.

I Give Hope

A specialized donation drive focused on providing psychosocial support and "Dignity Kits" to women and children in evacuation centers. This campaign ensures that those displaced by war or disaster maintain their health and self-respect.

Partnership for Disaster Resilience

A corporate-focused campaign that encourages private companies to adopt specific communities. Together, we provide disaster-readiness training and emergency supply kits to ensure businesses and their neighbors can survive major calamities.
