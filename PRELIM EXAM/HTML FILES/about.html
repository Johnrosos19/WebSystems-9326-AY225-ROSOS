Our Organization

The Philippine Red Cross (PRC) is the premier humanitarian organization in the country. Guided by the motto "Always First, Always Ready, Always There," we provide life-saving services and support to the most vulnerable Filipinos during disasters and daily emergencies.

Mission

To provide a premium quality humanitarian service in alleviating the suffering and uplifting the dignity of the most vulnerable people through well-trained staff and volunteers.

Vision

To be the foremost humanitarian organization in the Philippines that is capable of rapid response and delivery of services to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

Our History

Originally founded in 1947 through Republic Act No. 95, the Philippine Red Cross has roots dating back to the Philippine Revolution. What began as a service primarily focused on blood and disaster relief has evolved into a holistic humanitarian power-house, officially recognized as an independent and autonomous organization under the Philippine Red Cross Act of 2009 (RA 10072).

7 Fundamental Principles

Everything we do is guided by the seven fundamental principles of the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement:

Humanity

To prevent and alleviate human suffering wherever it may be found.

Impartiality

No discrimination as to nationality, race, religious beliefs, or class.

Neutrality

Not taking sides in hostilities or political controversies.

Independence

Maintaining autonomy while assisting government humanitarian services.

Voluntary Service

A voluntary relief movement not prompted in any manner by desire for gain.

Unity

There can be only one Red Cross Society in any one country.

Universality

All societies have equal status and share equal responsibilities.

What We Do

We operate through a network of over 100 chapters nationwide, providing six major services:

  • National Blood Services
  • Disaster Management
  • Safety Services (First Aid/CPR)
  • Health Services
  • Social Services
  • Red Cross Youth

Our Core Values

Our volunteers are driven by the spirit of:

  • Kusang Loob: Unwavering volunteerism.
  • Malasakit: Genuine compassion.
  • Kapwa Tao: Oneness with humanity.
