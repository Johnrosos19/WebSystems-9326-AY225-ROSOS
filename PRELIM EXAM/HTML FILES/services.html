How to Donate Blood

  • Step 1: Registration - Fill out the donor history questionnaire form at any Red Cross Blood Center.
  • Step 2: Weight & Vitals Check - Your weight, blood pressure, pulse, and temperature will be checked.
  • Step 3: Blood Screening - A small drop of blood will be taken to check your hemoglobin level and blood type.
  • Step 4: Physician's Interview - A doctor will review your history to ensure you are fit to donate.
  • Step 5: Extraction - The actual donation takes about 5 to 10 minutes, collecting 350ml to 450ml of blood.
  • Step 6: Recovery - Enjoy some light snacks and rest for 15 minutes before leaving the center.
